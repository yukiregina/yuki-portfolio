About

I grew up between two cultures that don't naturally speak to each other. That gap became my work: identifying what's missing before it reaches the screen.

I trained as a Graphic Designer in Tokyo, where restraint is a discipline. I built most of my career in São Paulo, where complexity is the starting point. Both shaped the same habit: slow down on the question, move fast on the answer.

From Art Direction to Product Design, every step pointed in the same direction. When design truly serves people, business growth follows.
